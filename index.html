100% cool vibes!

Deodorants mask odours, but Polar keeps you dry, cool, and fresh.There is only one option when it comes to a confident you.

Never doubt if you are truly fresh!

You can say goodbye to feeling those cold beads of sweat drip down your arm. With Polar, you can step into any situation confident you are 100% cool and fresh, even when things get a little more exciting!

This is how it works!

Salt and Alcohol

We have a secret formula that we use for our products, which combines salt and alcohol in a special way. This formula is what makes our spray and roller products so effective. We use a type of salt because it's a key ingredient that temporarily shuts off the sweat glands, keeping you feeling fresh and dry all day long. Not only that, but it also prevents bacteria from spreading, which is important for maintaining good hygiene.

Roll and Sleep

Roll on some Polarwise before bed and let it work its magic while you sleep. Remember to clean and dry your skin before application. Simply roll 1 or 2 times under your armpit or apply a thin layer with the roller, let it dry, then go to bed. Our formula keeps sweat inside the skin, so you can wake up feeling cool, fresh, and confident to take on the day ahead. It's that easy! :)

Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answer to your question below!

  • What are the ingredients?

    Strong:
    alcohol, aluminium chloride hexahydrate, denatonium benzoate, citrus aurantium bergamia fruit oil, MEK

    Original:
    alcohol, aqua, aluminium chlorohydrate, propylene glycol, denatonium benzoate, citrus aurantium bergamia fruit oil, MEK
  • Why do I need to apply this product before bed?

    Our product is effective because of the soluble salt dissolved in alcohol. The solution is absorbed into the skin at night and prevents sweat from reaching the skin surface. The salt naturally disappears after a few days, like the natural replacement of dead skin cells. That's why you only need to use Polar once in a few days.
